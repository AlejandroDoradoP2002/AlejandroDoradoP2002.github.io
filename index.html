How I Work

Whether I’m building a full-stack app, training a model, or wiring a server, I follow a similar process focused on clarity, reliability, and measurable outcomes.

1. Understand the problem

Start with the real workflow or pain point: who uses this, what “done” looks like, and what constraints we have (time, data, infra, security).

2. Design before coding

Sketch APIs, data models, and architecture. For larger work, I use UML, diagrams, and small design docs before touching the keyboard.

3. Build iteratively

Ship in small slices: get an end-to-end version working, then layer in tests, hardening, and performance improvements.

4. Measure & refine

Look at metrics (latency, errors, user behavior, time saved) and use them to decide what to improve next, instead of guessing.
