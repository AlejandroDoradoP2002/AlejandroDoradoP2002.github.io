AI JobShield – Fake Job Posting Detector

ML & NLP · Python · scikit-learn · Streamlit

AI JobShield analyzes more than 17,000 job postings and predicts whether they are likely to be fraudulent using NLP preprocessing and supervised learning. It exposes the model via a Streamlit UI so non-technical users can experiment with real postings.

  • Built a preprocessing pipeline for text normalization, tokenization, and vectorization.
  • Trained and evaluated models with accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score metrics.
  • Integrated the final model into a Streamlit app for interactive testing and demonstration.
